## 🚀 About ArcWorld

**ArcWorld.io** is an interactive platform designed to make exploring the **Arc ecosystem** more engaging, educational, and fun.

Instead of traditional documentation or static pages, ArcWorld transforms learning into an **interactive adventure** where users can explore through games, quizzes, puzzles, and discovery experiences.

The goal of the project is to make ecosystem knowledge **accessible, interactive, and memorable.**

---

## ✨ Features

🎮 **Mini Games**
Play interactive games inspired by the Arc ecosystem.

🧠 **Quizzes & Knowledge Challenges**
Test your understanding of the ecosystem through engaging quizzes.

🧩 **Puzzle Experiences**
Solve puzzles and discovery challenges designed to encourage exploration.

🌐 **Interactive Exploration**
Navigate through immersive ecosystem experiences.

📚 **Learning Hub**
Discover guides and educational resources that simplify complex concepts.

⚡ **Gamified Learning**
ArcWorld turns learning into an entertaining and rewarding experience.
